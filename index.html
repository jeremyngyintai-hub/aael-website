Basic Housing Unit certification簡樸房認證核證

New 2026 regime2026新制

The Basic Housing Unit (Regulation) Ordinance took effect on 1 March 2026. Every regulated subdivided unit must be certified by a "specified professional" as meeting the minimum living-environment standards before it can be lawfully let. Our in-house Registered Professional Surveyor (R.P.S., Building Surveying) is one of those specified professionals — so assessment, certification and any rectification works stay with one team. 《簡樸房條例》已於2026年3月1日生效。所有受規管的分間單位，必須經「指明專業人士」核證符合居住環境最低標準，方可合法出租。本公司駐司的註冊專業測量師（建築測量組別）正是條例指明的核證專業人士——由評估、核證以至改造工程，全程由同一團隊處理。

Why early matters more than it looks為何宜早不宜遲

Unlawful letting under the Ordinance carries heavy penalties, including a substantial fine and imprisonment. Beyond the law, there is a practical squeeze: with over a hundred thousand units in the market and a limited pool of specified professionals and contractors, the closer to the deadline, the harder it becomes to secure assessment and works slots. 根據條例，違法出租可招致嚴重罰則，包括高額罰款及監禁。除法律風險外，更實際的問題是資源擠塞：全港逾十萬個分間單位，而指明專業人士及承辦商數目有限，愈接近死線，愈難安排評估及工程檔期。

What does an Authorized Person (A.P.) actually do — and do I need one?認可人士（A.P.）究竟做甚麼？我需要嗎？
Under the Buildings Ordinance, most building works in Hong Kong must be prepared, submitted and supervised by an Authorized Person. The AP signs the statutory submissions, coordinates Buildings Department approval and carries the legal responsibility. In short: the BD requires a name on the drawings that takes responsibility — with us, that name is in-house, not borrowed. 根據《建築物條例》，香港大部分建築工程均須由認可人士草擬、入則及監督。AP須簽署法定文件、統籌屋宇署審批，並承擔法律責任。簡單而言：屋宇署要求圖則上有人承擔責任——在AAEL，這位簽署人是駐司專業人士，並非外借掛名。
