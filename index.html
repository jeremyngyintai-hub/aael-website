What does an Authorized Person (AP) actually do — and do I need one?認可人士（AP）究竟做甚麼？我需要嗎？
Under the Buildings Ordinance, most building works in Hong Kong must be prepared, submitted and supervised by an Authorized Person. The AP signs the statutory submissions, coordinates Buildings Department approval and carries the legal responsibility. In short: the BD requires a name on the drawings that takes responsibility — with us, that name is in-house, not borrowed. 根據《建築物條例》，香港大部分建築工程均須由認可人士草擬、入則及監督。AP須簽署法定文件、統籌屋宇署審批，並承擔法律責任。簡單而言：屋宇署要求圖則上有人承擔責任——在AAEL，這位簽署人是駐司專業人士，並非外借掛名。
I've received an MBIS notice. What should I do?收到強制驗樓通知，應如何處理？
Don't panic — the statutory deadline gives you months, not days. The steps: appoint a Registered Inspector, carry out the inspection, submit the report, and complete any required repairs. We handle all of it with Registered Professional Surveyors in-house. Send us a photo of the notice and we'll tell you exactly what applies to your building, free of charge. 無需驚慌——法定期限以月計算，並非以日計算。程序為：委任註冊檢驗人員、進行檢驗、提交報告，以及完成所需修葺。我們的駐司註冊檢驗人員及註冊建築測量師可全權辦理上述程序。只需將通知書拍照傳送給我們，我們將免費告知您的樓宇具體所需的跟進事項。
