簡樸房登記與認證時間表：業主四年路線圖BHU Registration and Certification: The Four-Year Roadmap

躍昇建築事務顧問有限公司　·　更新於 2026年7月Ascend Architecture & Engineering Ltd　·　Updated July 2026

條例給了業主合共四十八個月的過渡安排，聽起來很寬裕。但真正可用的時間，遠比帳面上少——原因在於評估、申請與工程無法同時進行。The Ordinance provides 48 months of transition, which sounds generous. The usable time is far shorter, because assessment, applications and works cannot all run in parallel.

四個關鍵日期

日期發生甚麼
2026年3月1日《簡樸房條例》生效。12個月登記期開始，同時接受認證申請。
2027年2月28日登記期結束。未登記的單位失去寬限期保障。
2027年3月1日開始執法。出租未登記及未獲認證的分間單位即屬犯罪。
2030年2月28日36個月寬限期結束。此後所有出租分間單位均須持有有效認證。

第一階段：登記期（現在）

現存分間單位的擁有人可為其樓宇單位申請寬限期登記。這一步是後續一切的前提：完成登記，才能取得其後36個月的寬限期，在此期間合法出租並進行改造。

錯過登記期的後果並非「遲些再補」——而是失去寬限期，須即時符合認證要求方可出租。這是整條時間線上最不能錯過的一個日子。

登記期內應完成的事

不只是遞交表格。理想做法是在登記的同時完成專業評估，掌握單位實際需要改造的項目及規模，讓其後三年的工程與資金安排有依據，而不是到寬限期中段才開始發現問題。

第二階段：寬限期（2027年3月起 36個月）

已登記單位可在此期間進行改造工程並申請認證。三年看似充足，但實務上須預留：

  • 專業評估及方案設計
  • 水電錶等公用事業申請（無法壓縮的行政時間）
  • 如涉及結構或間隔改動，可能需要的法定入則及審批
  • 實際施工，以及租戶安排（有租戶在住的單位施工安排更複雜）
  • 核證報告及認證申請處理時間

把這些串連起來，一個需要中度改造的單位由開始到取得認證，往往以季度計而非以星期計。手上有多個單位的業主，更要考慮施工檔期無法同時開展的問題。

第三階段：2030年3月之後

寬限期全面結束後，所有出租的分間單位均須持有有效認證。認證有效期為五年，其後須由指明專業人士重新核證方可續期——即是說，這不是一次性的工作，而是一個五年周期的合規管理。

為何「遲」的代價會遞增

除了法律風險，還有市場現實：全港有逾十萬個分間單位，而合資格的指明專業人士、承辦商及公用事業申請處理量都是有限的。愈接近死線，排期愈緊、選擇愈少、成本壓力愈大。早行動的業主，享有的不只是安心，還有議價空間與時間彈性。

本文為一般說明，實際日期及要求以房屋局最新公布為準。

Four key dates

DateWhat happens
1 Mar 2026Ordinance in force. Twelve-month registration period opens; certification applications accepted.
28 Feb 2027Registration period closes. Unregistered units lose grace-period protection.
1 Mar 2027Enforcement begins. Letting an unregistered, uncertified subdivided unit is an offence.
28 Feb 203036-month grace period ends. Every let subdivided unit must hold valid certification.

Stage one: the registration period (now)

Owners of existing subdivided units can apply for grace-period registration. This step is the precondition for everything after it: registration is what secures the following 36 months in which the unit can be let lawfully while works are carried out.

Missing the window is not a matter of catching up later — it means losing the grace period entirely and having to meet certification requirements immediately in order to let. It is the one date on this timeline that cannot be missed.

What to do during the registration period

More than filing a form. Ideally the professional assessment happens alongside registration, so the scale of works is known early and the next three years of programme and budget rest on facts rather than assumptions.

Stage two: the grace period (36 months from March 2027)

Registered units carry out works and obtain certification. Three years sounds ample; in practice it must absorb:

  • Professional assessment and scheme design
  • Utility applications for separate metering — administrative time that cannot be compressed
  • Statutory submissions and approval where structure or layout changes
  • The works themselves, plus tenant arrangements where units are occupied
  • Certification report and application processing

Strung together, a unit needing moderate works typically measures in quarters, not weeks. Owners holding several units face the further constraint that works cannot all start at once.

Stage three: after March 2030

Once the grace period ends, every let subdivided unit must hold valid certification. Certification lasts five years and renewal requires fresh certification by a specified professional — so this is a recurring compliance cycle, not a one-off exercise.

Why the cost of waiting compounds

Beyond legal risk there is market reality: over a hundred thousand units, and a finite supply of specified professionals, contractors and utility processing capacity. The closer to the deadline, the tighter the scheduling and the weaker the owner's negotiating position. Acting early buys flexibility, not just peace of mind.
