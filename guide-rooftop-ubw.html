天台屋與平台僭建：為何執法優先，業主如何處理Rooftop and Podium Structures: Why They Are Enforcement Priorities

躍昇建築事務顧問有限公司　·　更新於 2026年7月Ascend Architecture & Engineering Ltd　·　Updated July 2026

天台屋與平台僭建在香港存在多年，很多業主誤以為「一直冇人理」等於安全。實情是，這一類僭建正正屬於屋宇署的執法優先類別——因為它同時觸及走火通道、結構荷載與防水三個最敏感的問題。Rooftop and podium structures have existed for decades, and owners often read the absence of enforcement as safety. In fact this category sits high on the enforcement list, because it touches the three most sensitive issues at once: escape routes, structural loading and waterproofing.

為何這一類最受關注

走火通道

天台在許多樓宇是法定的緊急疏散空間。天台被搭建物佔用或分隔，火警時居民無處可退，後果直接關乎人命。這也是天台僭建幾乎沒有「從寬處理」空間的原因。

結構荷載

天台樓板的設計荷載，一般只考慮防水層、少量設備及維修人員通行，並非用來承托牆身、樓板及長期居住的重量。加建結構等於在設計以外加載，長遠影響樓板及支承構件。

防水

搭建工程往往需要鑽孔固定、開槽埋管，破壞原有防水層。結果是頂層單位滲漏——而滲漏又會加速鋼筋鏽蝕及石屎剝落，問題向下蔓延。

能否規範化

相比室內間隔改動，天台及平台搭建物可規範化的空間明顯較小。原因是它們通常難以同時滿足現行規例對總樓面面積、上蓋面積、樓宇高度、消防及疏散的要求。個別情況需按批准圖則、地契條款及實際設計評估，但業主宜以「須清拆」作為預設，再由專業人士確認是否有例外空間。

對業權及交易的影響

  • 命令登記——清拆令會在土地註冊處登記，成為業權上的負擔
  • 按揭——銀行估價及批核會考慮僭建情況，可能影響成數甚至拒批
  • 買賣——買方律師查冊必然發現，往往成為壓價或撻訂的理由
  • 保險——因未經批准構築物引致的損失，未必受保

與簡樸房認證的直接關係

一個關鍵限制

位於天台、平台、後巷、天井或庭院等位置的僭建物，不符合簡樸房認證的申請資格。這不是「改到合標準就可以」的問題，而是根本不在制度容許之列。持有這類單位而打算循簡樸房路線繼續出租的業主，應及早了解實際選項，避免投入無法回收的改造費用。

此外，即使您的分間單位本身位於合資格的樓宇單位內，若大廈的僭建物阻塞逃生通道，亦可能影響認證申請的審批。

處理的正確次序

  1. 先評估，後動工——由認可人士及註冊結構工程師檢視批准圖則與現場，確認性質及可行方案。自行拆除有機會破壞防水層或損及結構。
  2. 確定路線——清拆或（如可行）循法定途徑規範化。
  3. 由註冊承建商施工並由專業人士監督——尤其涉及防水層重做及結構還原。
  4. 完成防水及還原——清拆後的天台必須重新做好防水，否則問題只是換一種形式出現。
  5. 提交文件解除命令——確保紀錄正式清除。

本文為一般說明，不構成法律意見。個別情況須按批准圖則、地契及專業評估處理。

Why this category attracts attention

Means of escape

In many buildings the roof is a designated refuge space. Structures occupying or subdividing it leave residents nowhere to go in a fire. That is why there is very little latitude here.

Structural loading

Roof slabs are generally designed for waterproofing, limited plant and maintenance access — not for walls, floors and permanent occupation. Added structures load the slab and its supports beyond the design assumption.

Waterproofing

Fixing structures means drilling and chasing, which breaches the membrane. The result is leakage to the top floor — and leakage accelerates reinforcement corrosion and spalling, carrying the problem downwards.

Can they be regularised?

Far less often than internal alterations. Rooftop and podium structures rarely satisfy current requirements for gross floor area, site coverage, building height, fire safety and escape simultaneously. Each case turns on the approved plans, lease conditions and design, but owners should treat removal as the default and have a professional confirm whether any exception exists.

Effect on title and transactions

  • Registered orders become an encumbrance on title
  • Mortgage — lending and valuation are affected, sometimes refused
  • Sale — the buyer's search will find it, and it becomes a price or abort issue
  • Insurance — losses arising from unapproved structures may not be covered

Direct effect on BHU certification

A hard limit

Structures on rooftops, podiums, rear lanes, light wells or yards are not eligible for Basic Housing Unit certification. This is not a matter of upgrading to standard — such premises fall outside the scheme. Owners of these units planning to continue letting should understand their real options before spending on conversion.

Separately, even where your unit sits in an eligible building unit, unauthorised works obstructing escape routes elsewhere in the building can affect the application.

The right sequence

  1. Assess before you demolish — an Authorized Person and Registered Structural Engineer review the approved plans and site. Self-help demolition often damages the membrane or the structure.
  2. Fix the route — removal, or regularisation where genuinely available.
  3. Registered contractor, professional supervision, particularly for waterproofing and structural reinstatement.
  4. Reinstate the waterproofing — a stripped roof left unsealed simply changes the form of the problem.
  5. File the documents to discharge the order.
