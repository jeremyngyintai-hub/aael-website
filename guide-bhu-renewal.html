簡樸房認證五年續期：不是一次過的工作The Five-Year Renewal: Certification Is Not a One-Off

躍昇建築事務顧問有限公司　·　更新於 2026年7月Ascend Architecture & Engineering Ltd　·　Updated July 2026

簡樸房認證不是一勞永逸的證書，而是一個五年周期的合規安排。第一批取得認證的單位，最快在2030年第四季便要開始處理續期——而續期同樣需要指明專業人士重新核證。Certification is not a certificate you frame and forget. It is a five-year compliance cycle. The first certified units will begin renewing as early as the fourth quarter of 2030 — and renewal again requires certification by a specified professional.

續期的基本安排

  • 簡樸房認證有效期為五年
  • 續期申請須在有效期屆滿之前的三至六個月內提出
  • 續期須再次由指明專業人士核證單位仍然符合居住環境最低標準
  • 由於認證由二〇二六年三月一日起接受申請，首批獲認證的簡樸房最快可在二〇三〇年第四季起申請續期
「三至六個月」是一個窗口，不是提示

太早遞交不獲受理，太遲則可能出現認證中斷。而在認證失效期間出租，法律風險與未認證無異。因此續期不是到期前才處理的事，而是應該在到期前約一年就開始安排。

五年之間會發生甚麼

續期需要重新核證，正因為單位的狀況會隨時間改變。五年之間，常見的變化包括：

  • 租客搬入搬出、自行改動——加裝間隔、封窗、改動通風，都可能令單位不再符合標準
  • 設備老化——機械通風、消防裝置、電力裝置需要保養，失效即不合標準
  • 滲漏及結構劣化——尤其舊樓，五年足以令小問題變成大問題
  • 大廈層面的新問題——新的法定命令、公用部分損壞，同樣可能影響續期
  • 法例及實務守則更新——標準本身可能有調整

把五年當成一個維護周期

與其在到期前發現一堆問題，合理的做法是把五年拆成可管理的節奏：

  1. 取得認證後——完整保存核證報告、圖則、水電錶文件及認證文件。續期時這些是起點。
  2. 每年——簡單巡查一次：通風設備、消防裝置、去水、有否租客自行改動。發現得早，處理成本低。
  3. 租客更替時——交吉時同步檢查單位是否維持原狀，並在租約訂明不得擅自改動。
  4. 到期前約一年——安排專業預檢，確認是否仍然符合標準，預留時間處理發現的問題。
  5. 到期前三至六個月——正式提交續期申請。

租約條款值得預先處理

由於租客的改動可能直接影響續期，租約中宜訂明：不得擅自改動間隔、封閉窗戶、遮擋通風口或改動固定裝置；並保留合理時間安排檢驗及維修的入屋權利。這一條在續期時可能省下大量金錢與爭拗。

為何值得由同一團隊跟進

續期核證需要對比單位的原有狀況——原本的核證報告、圖則、當時的量度數據。若由原班人馬跟進，這些資料本身就在手；否則須重新測繪及建立紀錄，時間與成本都較高。

本文為一般說明，實際續期要求以《簡樸房條例》及房屋局最新公布為準。

How renewal works

  • Certification is valid for five years
  • Renewal must be applied for three to six months before expiry
  • Renewal requires fresh certification by a specified professional that the unit still meets the minimum standards
  • Since applications open on 1 March 2026, the earliest renewals fall due from the fourth quarter of 2030
"Three to six months" is a window, not a reminder

Apply too early and it will not be entertained; too late and the certification may lapse. Letting during a lapse carries the same legal exposure as never having been certified. Renewal planning should begin roughly a year ahead.

What changes in five years

  • Tenants come and go, and alter things — added partitions, blocked windows, modified ventilation
  • Equipment ages — mechanical ventilation, fire and electrical installations need maintenance; failed equipment means non-compliance
  • Leakage and deterioration — five years is ample for a small defect to become a large one
  • New building-level issues — fresh statutory orders or damaged common parts
  • Regulatory updates — the standards themselves may be revised

Treat the five years as a maintenance cycle

  1. On certification — keep the report, drawings, metering documents and certificate together. Renewal starts from these.
  2. Annually — a short walk-through: ventilation, fire installations, drainage, tenant alterations.
  3. At each tenancy change — check the unit is as certified, and write a no-alteration clause into the tenancy.
  4. About a year before expiry — a professional pre-check, leaving time to fix what it finds.
  5. Three to six months before expiry — lodge the renewal.

Tenancy clauses worth having

Because tenant alterations can directly affect renewal, tenancies should prohibit altering partitions, blocking windows or ventilation and modifying fixed installations, and should reserve reasonable access for inspection and repair. That clause can save considerable money and argument at renewal.

Why continuity matters

Renewal certification is a comparison against the unit's original condition — the first report, the drawings, the measurements taken at the time. Where the same practice continues, that record is already in hand; otherwise it has to be rebuilt from scratch, in both time and cost.
